Hello World!

Hello, my name is Alejo Geronimo Tomé, I'm 24 years old and I'm an Argentinean web designer and front end developer. I consider myself a hard-working person, I'm persistent and responsible, I'm always open to learning and growing in my career path. I tend to prefer working by myself, but I function really well in working groups, even though I don't consider myself as a leader. Back at high school I started in this world studying to be a car programmer, I didn't followed that path, but I keep studying by myself. At first I became interested in and studied to be a video game programmer; in 2016 I discovered cyber security and pen testing, but in that time I didn't pursued the studies that long because I was working somewhere else. In the pandemic, my interest for programming world was reborn as a consequence of the isolation, that's when, slowly but surely, I started the path of the front end developing and web designing studies. To see my certifications and insignia, I invite you to see that part of my page. As my short term aspiration, I'm looking for a freelance web designing and front end development job, as I keep studying and professionalizing myself in other related areas of interest.
